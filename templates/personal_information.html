Personal Information Collection Statement
收集個人資料聲明

We value your privacy and are committed to handling your personal information in accordance with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance of Hong Kong. When using AIR Lab GenAI SIG Face Swapper, our data processing procedures are as follows:

Purpose of Data Collection: The personal photos and facial information you provide will only be used to generate AI face-swapped images and will not be used for any other purposes.
Data Processing and Use: All data processing is conducted locally on this device, ensuring that your data is not uploaded to the internet during the process.
Data Transfer: When you choose to download the generated images, they will be uploaded to our private Imgur album. The uploaded images are only used to provide the download service and will not be displayed to any third party.
Data Retention and Destruction: All images stored locally and those uploaded to the Imgur album will be automatically destroyed within one week after the event ends.
